Popular Destinations

Paris

Paris is known for its gorgeous, imposing monuments. These iconic structures, often an exemplar of a particular era in architecture, are one of the city's instantly recognizable elements. The preeminent of Paris's landmarks is the Eiffel Tower.

Tokyo

It is renowned for its historic shrines and temples, as well as traditional performing arts such as Noh, Kabuki, and Rakugo, which have been cherished for centuries. Prominent venues celebrating Japanese cultural traditions include the Kabukiza Theatre, the National Noh Theatre, and the Kokugikan Hall.

Cape-Town

Famous for its sun, sea and scenery, Cape Town is a beloved tourist destination for visitors the world over. It's, therefore, no surprise that this coastal gem has been voted the best city in the world seven-years-running!
